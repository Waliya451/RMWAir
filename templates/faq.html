FAQ

Navigating Your Journey with Ease

Answers to Your Travel Queries
QUESTION: 1
Q: How can I make a reservation?
A: Making a reservation is quick and easy. You can book your flight online through
our website,or you can contact our dedicated reservation team via phone or email
for personalized assistance.
QUESTION: 2
Q: What is the baggage allowance?
A: The baggage allowance may vary depending on your ticket type and destination.
We provide specific guidelines regarding baggage weight and dimensions on our
website. Feel free to check our baggage policy or contact our customer service
for further details.
QUESTION: 3
Q: Can I make changes to my booking?
A: Yes, you can make changes to your booking, such as modifying the travel dates
or passenger details. However, please note that certain restrictions and fees
may apply. We recommend contacting our customer service or visiting our Manage
Booking section on the website to make any necessary changes.
QUESTION: 4
Q: What are the check-in options?
A: We offer convenient check-in options to suit your preference. You can check in
online through our website or mobile app, or you can check in at the airport
counter. Online check-in is available within a specified time frame before your
flight departure.
QUESTION: 5
Q: What measures are in place for passenger safety and security?
A: Your safety and security are our top priorities. We adhere to stringent safety
standards and protocols set by aviation authorities. We have implemented
comprehensive safety measures, including enhanced cleaning procedures, cabin
air filtration systems, and contactless procedures to ensure a safe and
comfortable journey for all passengers.
