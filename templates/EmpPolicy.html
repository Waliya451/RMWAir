POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

Setting the Standard: Policies That Drive Excellence

Learn more about our Guiding Principles for Success and Compliance
RMW AIRLINES is committed to prioritizing the safety of its passengers, crew, and aircraft.
Stringent adherence to national and international aviation safety regulations is a
fundamental aspect of the company's operations. This includes conducting regular safety
training and drills to ensure preparedness for emergency situations. Additionally,
RMW AIRLINES maintains a strong focus on customer service, emphasizing professionalism,
courtesy, and attentiveness to meet passengers' needs. The company provides timely and
accurate information regarding flight schedules and delays, and actively addresses
customer complaints and feedback.
Security is a paramount concern for RMW AIRLINES. The company implements rigorous
access control measures for restricted areas, while baggage and passenger screening
protocols adhere to strict security standards. RMW Airlines places a strong emphasis on
environmental sustainability, implementing measures to reduce fuel consumption, manage
waste, and minimize noise pollution.
In terms of operations, RMW AIRLINES follows detailed procedures for flight planning,
dispatch, and monitoring. The airline also places significant importance on aircraft
maintenance, adhering to manufacturer recommendations and regulatory requirements.
Crew management is carefully managed, ensuring proper scheduling, duty hours, rest
periods, and ongoing training. Ground operations, such as check-in, baggage handling, and
boarding, are executed with efficiency and passenger satisfaction in mind.
Financial stability is a key focus for RMW AIRLINES, following sound financial practices and
maintaining transparency in accounting and reporting. Human resources policies are
designed to attract and retain qualified employees, emphasizing equal employment
opportunities, performance evaluation, compensation, and benefits. Crisis management
procedures are established to effectively handle emergency situations, ensuring the safety
and well-being of passengers, crew, and aircraft.
RMW AIRLINES maintains a commitment to safety, customer service, efficient
operations, security, environmental sustainability, financial stability, and employee well-being
These policies and procedures form the foundation for the company's operations,
ensuring a high standard of service and compliance with industry regulations.
